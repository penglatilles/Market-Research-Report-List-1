<p><p>The fibrillated polypropylene concrete fiber market comprises two primary types: monofilament fiber and multifilament fiber. Monofilament fibers are single, continuous strands that enhance concrete's toughness and crack resistance, offering effective reinforcement. In contrast, multifilament fibers consist of multiple filaments twisted together, providing superior distribution and performance in controlling shrinkage cracks and improving durability. Both types contribute significantly to enhancing the mechanical properties and longevity of concrete structures, catering to various applications within construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
