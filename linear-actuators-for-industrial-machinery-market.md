<p><p>Linear actuators are essential components in industrial machinery, providing movement and control. The three primary types are pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic actuators. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air for motion, offering rapid response and lightweight design, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Electric actuators, driven by motors, provide precise control, easy integration, and energy efficiency. Hydraulic actuators utilize pressurized fluid for heavy-duty applications, delivering significant force and power, suitable for lifting and moving large loads in industrial settings.</p></p>
