<p><p>Back-illuminated GS CMOS sensors are advanced imaging sensors designed to enhance light sensitivity and image quality, particularly in low-light environments. As a result, applications in professional and consumer cameras, smartphones, automotive imaging, and medical devices are expanding.</p><p>The Back-illuminated GS CMOS Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
