<p><strong>The Variable Displacement Vane Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Operated</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable displacement vane pumps are categorized into direct operated and pilot operated types based on their control mechanisms. Direct operated pumps adjust displacement through a mechanical linkage, responding directly to load conditions, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, pilot operated pumps utilize a secondary control system, often hydraulic, to regulate displacement indirectly, allowing for more precise control and efficiency under varying load conditions. Both types serve various industrial applications, optimizing performance and energy use in fluid systems.</p></p>
