<p><p>The RTU (Ready-to-Use) Porcelain Enamel market comprises two primary types: enamel powder and enamel frit. Enamel powder is a fine, dry mixture of glass and color pigments used to create a smooth, durable finish when fired. Enamel frit, on the other hand, is a pre-melted glass product that is ground into a fine powder. Both types serve different applications in industries like cookware, appliances, and automotive, providing resistance, aesthetic appeal, and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
