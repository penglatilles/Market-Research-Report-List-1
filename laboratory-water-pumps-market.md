<p><p>The laboratory water pumps market can be categorized into three main types: electric, magnetic-drive, and pneumatic pumps. Electric pumps are powered by electricity, offering precise control and consistent flow rates, making them ideal for various applications. Magnetic-drive pumps use a magnetic coupling to transfer motion, eliminating leaks and enhancing safety in sensitive environments. Pneumatic pumps operate using compressed air, providing high efficiency and portability, suitable for applications where electrical equipment may not be permissible or practical.</p></p>
