<p><ul><li>Single Stage Volute Pumps</li><li>Multistage Volute Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic volute pumps are categorized into two main types: single-stage and multistage volute pumps. Single-stage volute pumps consist of one impeller and are ideal for low-pressure applications, providing high flow rates and straightforward designs. In contrast, multistage volute pumps contain multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressures and effectively handle various applications, including water supply and industrial processes. Both types are designed for durability and efficiency, making them suitable for environments requiring robust pumping solutions.</p></p>
