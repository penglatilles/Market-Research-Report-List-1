<p><p>Botulism illness market is segmented into three primary types: antibiotics, antitoxins, and other treatments. Antibiotics are utilized to manage secondary infections caused by botulinum toxin, although they do not counteract the toxin itself. Antitoxins, including botulinum antitoxin, are critical for neutralizing toxins and preventing disease progression. The "other" category encompasses supportive therapies and emerging treatments, such as immunoglobulins and respiratory assistance, aimed at providing symptomatic relief and aiding recovery from the effects of botulism.</p></p>
