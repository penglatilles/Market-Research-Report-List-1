<p><p>The Open-Angle Glaucoma Therapeutics market includes several key types. Prostaglandin Analogs (PGAs) are the most commonly prescribed treatments, enhancing aqueous humor outflow to reduce intraocular pressure. Combination therapies often involve PGAs paired with other medications to enhance efficacy while minimizing side effects. Additionally, the "Other" market encompasses various treatment options such as beta-blockers, alpha agonists, and carbonic anhydrase inhibitors, which target different mechanisms to manage intraocular pressure in glaucoma patients effectively.</p></p>
