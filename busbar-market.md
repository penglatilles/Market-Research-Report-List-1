<p><p>A busbar is a conductive material, typically made of copper or aluminum, used to distribute electricity within electrical circuits and systems. It serves as a central point for the connection and distribution of power, facilitating efficient transmission between various components of the electrical infrastructure. Busbars are integral in substations, switchgear, and large industrial installations, providing a compact way to manage high voltage and large currents.</p><p>The busbar market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
