<p><p>The functional sugar market comprises various types, including oligosaccharides, inulin, sugar alcohols, and others. Oligosaccharides are short-chain carbohydrates offering prebiotic benefits, enhancing gut health. Inulin, a type of soluble fiber, aids digestion and supports weight management. Sugar alcohols, such as xylitol and erythritol, provide sweetness with fewer calories and lower glycemic impact, making them popular in low-sugar products. Other emerging functional sugars include natural sweeteners and various fibers, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking alternative sweetening options.</p></p>
