<p><p>The Smart Gas Meter System market is primarily categorized into Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems enable one-way communication, automatically collecting meter data for utility billing without manual intervention. In contrast, AMI offers two-way communication, allowing utilities to monitor real-time consumption, manage demand, and enhance customer engagement. This advanced infrastructure supports detailed analytics and interactive services, improving efficiency and accuracy in gas management, ultimately benefiting both utilities and consumers.</p></p>
