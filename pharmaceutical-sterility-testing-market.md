<p><p>The pharmaceutical sterility testing market encompasses several key types essential for ensuring product safety. Sterility testing verifies the absence of viable microorganisms in sterile products, crucial for parenteral formulations. Bioburden testing assesses the initial microbial load in raw materials and finished products, guiding contamination control measures. Bacterial endotoxin testing detects harmful endotoxins produced by bacteria, safeguarding patient safety by ensuring products meet safety standards. These testing methods collectively ensure the integrity and safety of pharmaceutical products in a highly regulated industry.</p></p>
