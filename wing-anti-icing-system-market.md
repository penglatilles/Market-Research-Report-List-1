<p><strong>The Wing Anti-icing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical De-icing System</li><li>Electric Pulse Anti-icing System</li><li>Electric Heating Anti-icing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wing Anti-icing System market comprises three primary types: Mechanical De-icing Systems use pneumatic boots that inflate to shed ice from the wing surface; Electric Pulse Anti-icing Systems employ rapid electrical pulses to disrupt ice formation; and Electric Heating Anti-icing Systems utilize electrical heaters embedded in the wing structure to maintain temperatures above freezing, preventing ice accumulation. Each system is designed to enhance aircraft safety and performance during adverse weather conditions, ensuring reliable operations in icy environments.</p></p>
