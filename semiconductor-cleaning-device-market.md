<p><ul><li>Monolithic Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Tank Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor cleaning device market consists primarily of two types: monolithic cleaning equipment and tank cleaning equipment. Monolithic cleaning equipment integrates all functions into a single unit, optimizing space and efficiency for wafer cleaning. This type is preferred for its compact design and ease of use in cleanroom environments. Tank cleaning equipment, on the other hand, utilizes tanks filled with cleaning solutions to process multiple wafers simultaneously, making it ideal for batch processing and large-scale production settings. Both types are essential for maintaining the cleanliness required in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
