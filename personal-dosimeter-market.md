<p><strong>The Personal Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pen Dosimeters</li><li>Direct Read Electronic Dosimeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The personal dosimeter market comprises two primary types: pen dosimeters and direct read electronic dosimeters. Pen dosimeters are compact, portable devices that measure radiation exposure and typically feature a scale for manual reading, making them user-friendly for various applications. Direct read electronic dosimeters, on the other hand, provide real-time measurements of radiation levels, offering instant feedback through digital displays. Both types serve crucial roles in monitoring radiation exposure for workers in healthcare, industry, and research settings, ensuring safety and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
