<p><p>The specialty sealants market comprises several key types, including silicone, silyl-modified, polyurethane, and polysulfides. Silicone sealants are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for various applications. Silyl-modified sealants combine the advantages of silicone and polyurethane, offering strong adhesion and weather resistance. Polyurethane sealants provide excellent mechanical properties and adhesion, suitable for dynamic joints. Polysulfide sealants are resistant to various chemicals and are often used in demanding environments, providing effective sealing and flexibility.</p></p>
