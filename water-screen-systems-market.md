<p><ul><li>Traveling Water Screen System</li><li>Static Water Screen System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water screen systems are essential in water treatment and industrial processes for filtering debris and aquatic life. The Traveling Water Screen System employs a rotating mesh screen that continually sifts through water, removing unwanted materials while allowing clean water to pass. In contrast, the Static Water Screen System features a fixed screen where water flows through, capturing contaminants without mechanical movement. Both types are crucial for maintaining water quality and protecting equipment in various applications, including power generation and wastewater management.</p></p>
