<p><strong>The Biorefinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biochemical</li><li>Thermochemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biorefinery markets can be categorized into biochemical and thermochemical types. The biochemical market focuses on converting biomass into bio-based products using biological processes, such as fermentation and enzymatic reactions, to produce fuels, chemicals, and materials. In contrast, the thermochemical market involves the use of heat and chemical processes, such as gasification and pyrolysis, to transform biomass into syngas, bio-oils, and char, suitable for energy generation and chemical feedstocks. Both approaches aim to promote sustainable resource utilization and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
