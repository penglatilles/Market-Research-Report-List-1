<p><p>The radial tire mold market includes two primary types: segmented molds and two-piece molds. Segmented molds are composed of multiple sections that can be assembled and disassembled, allowing for flexibility in production and easier maintenance. This type is often used for complex tire designs. In contrast, two-piece molds consist of a top and bottom half, providing a simpler structure for manufacturing standard tire shapes. Both types cater to different production needs, with segmented molds offering customization and two-piece molds emphasizing efficiency.</p></p>
