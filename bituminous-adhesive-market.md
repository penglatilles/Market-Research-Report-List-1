<p><strong>The Bituminous Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Applied</li><li>Hot Applied</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bituminous Adhesive Market includes two primary types: Cold Applied and Hot Applied. Cold Applied adhesives are ready-to-use, requiring no heating, making them convenient and safer for indoor applications. They cure at ambient temperatures, offering flexibility and ease of use. Hot Applied adhesives, on the other hand, require heating to achieve a liquid state before application. They provide superior bonding strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty and outdoor applications. Both types cater to construction, roofing, and waterproofing needs, with choice depending on project requirements.</p></p>
