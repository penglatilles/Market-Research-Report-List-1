<p><p>The rotational moulding equipment market comprises various machine types, each serving specific production needs. Rock and roll machines utilize a rocking motion for uniform material distribution, ideal for large parts. Shuttle machines feature a moving platform for efficient cycle times, suitable for medium-sized products. Carousel machines have rotating stations for simultaneous loading and cooling, enhancing productivity. Clamshell machines involve two halves that close around the mould, offering precision for complex designs. Other machines include specialized units tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
