<p><p>The bio-based pesticide inert ingredient market is categorized into solid and liquid types. Solid pesticide inert ingredients include substances like clays and natural minerals that serve as carriers or fillers, enhancing the effectiveness of active ingredients. Liquid pesticide inert ingredients often consist of oils, surfactants, or emulsifiers that improve application and absorption, facilitating better distribution and coverage on target plants. Both types support sustainable agricultural practices by reducing chemical load and enhancing the overall safety and efficacy of pest control solutions.</p></p>
