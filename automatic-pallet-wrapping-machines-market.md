<p><p>Automatic pallet wrapping machines are essential for securing goods on pallets, enhancing load stability during transportation. They come in two primary types: single-arm and double-arm machines. Single-arm machines utilize one rotating arm to wrap the stretch film around the pallet, ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, double-arm machines feature two rotating arms that provide faster wrapping speeds and higher efficiency, making them suitable for larger production demands. Both types improve productivity and reduce labor costs in packaging processes.</p></p>
