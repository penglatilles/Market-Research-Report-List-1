<p><p>The aerial ropeway market primarily comprises two types: detachable chairlifts and fixed grip chairlifts. Detachable chairlifts feature chairs that can disconnect from the cable at loading and unloading stations, allowing for smoother access and faster operation. In contrast, fixed grip chairlifts have chairs permanently attached to the cable, providing a steady ride, though they may operate at slower speeds. Both types serve to transport passengers efficiently in mountainous regions, ski resorts, and urban settings, enhancing accessibility and tourism.</p></p>
