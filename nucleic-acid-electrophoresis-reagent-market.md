<p><ul><li>Gel Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nucleic acid electrophoresis reagent market is primarily divided into two types: gel electrophoresis and capillary electrophoresis. Gel electrophoresis employs a gel matrix to separate nucleic acids based on size, allowing for the visualization and analysis of DNA or RNA fragments. In contrast, capillary electrophoresis utilizes narrow capillaries filled with a gel or polymer to achieve higher resolution and faster separation of nucleic acids. Both methods are essential in molecular biology, genomics, and diagnostics for analyzing genetic materials.</p></p>
