<p><strong>The Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The balloon catheter market comprises several types tailored for specific medical applications. Normal balloon catheters are used for vessel dilation. Cutting balloon catheters feature blades for incising plaque, enhancing efficacy during angioplasty. Scoring balloon catheters have defined scoring patterns that facilitate plaque fracture without extensive vessel trauma. Drug eluting balloon catheters deliver therapeutic agents to prevent restenosis. Stent graft balloon catheters are specially designed for deploying stent grafts in vascular interventions, ensuring optimal placement and function. Each type plays a critical role in vascular management.</p></p>
