<p><ul><li>Gap Fillers</li><li>Structural Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive assembly line adhesives market includes various types, primarily gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers are used to bond irregular surfaces and eliminate spaces, enhancing durability and sealing. These adhesives prevent moisture ingress and improve noise reduction. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, provide robust bonding strength critical for the integrity of vehicle components, like chassis and body panels. Both types are essential for ensuring vehicle performance, safety, and longevity in modern automotive manufacturing processes.</p></p>
