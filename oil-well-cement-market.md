<p><p>The Oil-Well Cement market comprises three primary types: Ordinary, Moderate Sulfate-Resistant, and High Sulfate-Resistant cements. Ordinary cement is used for general purposes, providing basic sealing and support in well construction. Moderate Sulfate-Resistant cement offers improved durability in environments with moderate sulfate exposure, suitable for specific geological conditions. High Sulfate-Resistant cement is designed for extreme sulfate environments, ensuring enhanced longevity and structural integrity in challenging conditions. Each type is tailored to meet specific environmental challenges in oil well applications.</p></p>
