<p><p>Microwave Integrated Circuits (MIC) are essential for high-frequency applications in telecommunications and radar systems. The market is primarily categorized into two types: Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits (MMIC) and Hybrid Microwave Integrated Circuits (HMIC). MMICs are fabricated on a single semiconductor substrate, offering compactness and efficiency, while HMICs combine different materials or technologies, allowing for greater design flexibility and performance tuning. Both types play critical roles in improving signal quality and enhancing system performance in various applications.</p></p>
