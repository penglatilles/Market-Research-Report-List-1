<p><p>The 4-inch silicon carbide substrate market can be categorized into two main types based on conductivity: conductive and semi-insulated. </p><p>Conductive substrates enable efficient charge transport, making them ideal for power electronics and high-frequency applications. </p><p>Semi-insulated substrates, on the other hand, have controlled conductivity, providing electrical isolation while still allowing some charge flow. This characteristic is beneficial for reducing parasitic capacitance in high-voltage devices. Both types play vital roles in enhancing the performance and reliability of advanced electronic systems.</p></p>
