<p><p>Transient Blocking Units (TBU) are essential components used in telecommunications and electrical systems to protect equipment from transient voltage spikes. The market for TBUs consists of two main types: bidirectional and unidirectional transient blocking units. Bidirectional TBUs can mitigate voltage spikes in both directions, making them suitable for applications where power flows can reverse, while unidirectional TBUs only guard against surges in one direction, typically used in systems with a consistent power flow. Both types ensure enhanced system reliability and longevity.</p></p>
