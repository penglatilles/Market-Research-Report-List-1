<p><p>The thermoplastic polyester market primarily consists of three types: PET, PBT, and PETP. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is widely used for packaging and fibers due to its excellent strength and recyclability. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) is favored for electrical and automotive applications due to its superior heat resistance and chemical stability. PETP (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) offers enhanced thermal properties and is ideal for high-performance applications, including engineering components. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving demand across various sectors.</p></p>
