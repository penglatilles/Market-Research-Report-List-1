<p><p>The laser cleaning head market can be divided into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed laser systems. Continuous Wave lasers emit a constant beam of light, providing effective and efficient cleaning for large surface areas or for applications requiring steady energy levels. In contrast, Pulsed lasers deliver energy in short bursts, allowing for precise control and the ability to remove contaminants from delicate surfaces without damaging them. Both types cater to various industrial cleaning needs, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
