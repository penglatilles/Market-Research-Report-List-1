Collagen gel/hydrogel offers a malleable, moisture-retaining medium ideal for delivering therapeutic agents and enhancing tissue hydration. Collagen pellets serve as compact, solid forms ideal for targeted delivery in various medical applications. Together, these products facilitate healing and tissue repair in regenerative medicine.</p></p>
