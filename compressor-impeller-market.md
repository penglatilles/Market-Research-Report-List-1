<p><strong>The Compressor Impeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Style</li><li>Semi-open Style</li><li>Closed Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor impeller market is categorized into three main types: open style, semi-open style, and closed style. Open impellers feature blades directly attached to a hub, providing high efficiency and ease of cleaning but lower pressure capabilities. Semi-open impellers combine features of open and closed styles, offering moderate efficiency and versatility for various applications. Closed impellers have blades enclosed within a shroud, delivering high efficiency and pressure rise, making them ideal for demanding industrial applications with controlled flow rates.</p></p>
