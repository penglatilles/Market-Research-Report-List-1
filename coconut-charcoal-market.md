<p><ul><li>Lump</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coconut charcoal market is primarily divided into two types: lump and powder. Lump coconut charcoal is produced from carbonized coconut shells, providing a natural and eco-friendly fuel source widely used in grilling and barbecuing due to its high heat retention and minimal smoke. In contrast, powdered coconut charcoal is finely ground and often utilized in various applications, including cosmetics, water filtration, and health supplements, thanks to its high adsorption capacity and detoxifying properties. Both types offer sustainable alternatives to traditional charcoal.</p></p>
