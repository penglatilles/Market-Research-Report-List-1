<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night vision goggles market comprises mainly two types: image intensifier and thermal image systems. Image intensifier goggles amplify existing light, allowing users to see in low-light conditions by enhancing visible light. In contrast, thermal image goggles detect infrared radiation, enabling users to visualize heat patterns even in total darkness. Both technologies are extensively used in military, law enforcement, security, and outdoor activities, each catering to specific operational needs depending on environmental conditions and visibility requirements.</p></p>
