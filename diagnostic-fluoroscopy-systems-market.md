<p><p>Diagnostic fluoroscopy systems are categorized into two main types: systems with C-arms and systems with tables. C-arm systems are portable and provide real-time imaging, making them ideal for interventional procedures and operating rooms. They allow flexibility in positioning for various angles. In contrast, table-based systems are typically larger and stationary, designed for comprehensive examinations and diagnostics. These systems generally include integrated tables that facilitate patient positioning and enhance imaging quality for a wide range of medical applications.</p></p>
