<p><p>The laminate tubes market is primarily divided into two categories: Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) tubes and Aluminum Barrier Laminate (ABL) tubes. PBL tubes are made of layers of plastics, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture and air, making them ideal for cosmetic and personal care products. ABL tubes, on the other hand, consist of aluminum layers combined with plastic, providing superior protection against light and chemical interaction, making them suitable for pharmaceuticals and food products. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
