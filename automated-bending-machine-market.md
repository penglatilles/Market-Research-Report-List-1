<p><p>The automated bending machine market consists of three primary types: hydraulic, electric, and mechanical bending machines. Hydraulic bending machines utilize fluid pressure to achieve precise bending, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Electric bending machines are driven by electrical motors, offering high efficiency and accuracy for lighter materials. Mechanical bending machines rely on a system of levers and gears to create bends, often favored for their simplicity and durability in various industrial settings. Each type serves specific operational needs within the metalworking industry.</p></p>
