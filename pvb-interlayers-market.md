<p><p>The PVB interlayers market primarily consists of two types: standard polyvinyl butyral (PVB) and structural polyvinyl butyral. Standard PVB is widely used for automotive and architectural applications due to its excellent transparency, adhesion, and safety features, providing effective sound and UV protection. Structural PVB, on the other hand, offers enhanced strength and load-bearing capabilities, making it suitable for applications in high-performance laminated glass, such as in buildings and bridges. Both types enhance safety and durability in various industries.</p></p>
