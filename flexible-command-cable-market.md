<p><p>The Flexible Command Cable market is divided into two main types: Single Core and Multi-core cables. Multi-core cables are often favored in sophisticated systems requiring simultaneous connections, whereas Single Core options are utilized for basic, less demanding installations, providing tailored solutions for various operational needs.</p></p>
