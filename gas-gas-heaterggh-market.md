<p><p>Gas Gas Heaters (GGH) are devices that recover waste heat from industrial processes to improve energy efficiency. The market is segmented into regenerative and rotary types. Regenerative GGHs use thermal storage to absorb and release heat, enhancing efficiency in cyclical processes. Rotary GGHs incorporate rotating heat exchangers to transfer heat between gas streams, suitable for continuous operations. Other types may include fixed-bed or shell-and-tube designs, catering to diverse application needs across industries for sustainable energy management.</p></p>
