<p><strong>The Touch Screens in Mobile Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Touch Screen</li><li>Resistive Touch Screen</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave Touch Screen</li><li>Infrared Touch Screens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touch screens in mobile devices can be categorized into several types. Capacitive touch screens utilize the electrical properties of the human body, allowing for multi-touch capabilities and high sensitivity. Resistive touch screens consist of two flexible layers that register pressure, making them suitable for stylus or gloved use. Surface acoustic wave touch screens use ultrasonic waves to detect touch, providing clear images. Infrared touch screens rely on infrared light beams, offering durability and responsiveness but are less common in mobile devices.</p></p>
