<p><p>Metal polishing services encompass three primary market types: mechanical, chemistry, and electrochemistry. </p><p>Mechanical polishing involves physical processes like sanding or grinding to achieve a smooth finish. Chemical polishing utilizes specialized solutions to remove surface imperfections and enhance gloss without abrasion. Electrochemical polishing employs an electrolytic solution to dissolve a thin layer of material, resulting in a highly polished surface with improved corrosion resistance. Each method caters to different needs, balancing efficiency, finish quality, and material compatibility.</p></p>
