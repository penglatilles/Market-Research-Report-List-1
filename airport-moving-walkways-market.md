<p><p>The airport moving walkways market is primarily divided into two types: belt-type and pallet-type systems. Belt-type walkways use a continuous belt to transport passengers, offering a smooth ride and easier maintenance. They are often preferred for shorter distances. On the other hand, pallet-type walkways consist of flat, platform-like surfaces providing stability and enhanced safety, especially for longer distances. Both types serve to improve passenger flow and convenience within busy airports, reducing transit time and enhancing the travel experience.</p></p>
