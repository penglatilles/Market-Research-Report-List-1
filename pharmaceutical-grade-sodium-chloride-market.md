<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Grade Sodium Chloride market is segmented into two primary types: API-NaCl and HD-NaCl. API-NaCl refers to sodium chloride that meets stringent regulatory standards for use in active pharmaceutical ingredients, ensuring high purity and quality for drug formulation. HD-NaCl, or High Dose NaCl, is used in formulations requiring higher concentrations of sodium chloride, often for intravenous applications. Both types play critical roles in healthcare, dietary applications, and various pharmaceutical processes, driving demand in the market.</p></p>
