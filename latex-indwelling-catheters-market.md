<p><p>The latex indwelling catheters market comprises various types, each serving specific medical needs. Indwelling urinary catheters facilitate urine drainage from the bladder, commonly used in patients with urinary retention. Indwelling plural catheters are designed for drainage of fluid from the pleural space, often utilized in managing pleural effusions. Indwelling peritoneal catheters support peritoneal dialysis for patients with renal failure. Indwelling nephrostomy catheters bypass obstructions, allowing drainage from the kidneys directly. These products are vital for improving patient comfort and outcomes.</p></p>
