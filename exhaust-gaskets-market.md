<p><strong>The Exhaust Gaskets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Manifold Gaskets</li><li>Exhaust Flange Gaskets</li><li>Header Gaskets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Exhaust Gaskets market includes various types designed for specific applications. **Exhaust Manifold Gaskets** seal the connection between the engine and exhaust manifold, preventing leaks and ensuring efficient exhaust flow. **Exhaust Flange Gaskets** are used between exhaust pipe connections, providing a tight seal at joints to prevent gas leakage. **Header Gaskets** are specialized for high-performance vehicles, sealing the headers to the engine block, enhancing exhaust flow and performance. Each type ensures durability, heat resistance, and leak-free operation in automotive exhaust systems.</p></p>
