<p><p>The full body harness market includes two primary types: belay loops and adjustable loops. The belay loop is a fixed connection point that enhances safety during climbing by allowing effective load distribution and easy attachment to ropes. In contrast, adjustable loops offer flexibility, enabling users to modify the fit for comfort and security. These harnesses are essential for various activities, including climbing, industrial work, and rescue operations, ensuring both safety and comfort for users in demanding environments.</p></p>
