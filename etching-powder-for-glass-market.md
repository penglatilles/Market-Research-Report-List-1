<p><p>The glass etching powder market consists of two primary types: waterborne glass etching powder and acid glass etching powder. Waterborne glass etching powder is characterized by its eco-friendly formulation, using water as a solvent, which minimizes harmful emissions during the etching process. Conversely, acid glass etching powder employs acidic compounds that chemically etch the glass surface, offering more aggressive and quicker results. Both types cater to different applications, with varying levels of safety, efficiency, and environmental impact in glass decoration and customization.</p></p>
