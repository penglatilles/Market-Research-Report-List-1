<p><p>The Molecular Spectroscopy Instruments Market encompasses various techniques used for analyzing molecular structures and compositions. Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) measures light absorption in the near-infrared range, ideal for organic compounds. Fourier-Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR) provides detailed information on molecular vibrations and functional groups. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) utilizes magnetic fields to elucidate molecular interactions and environments. Ultraviolet-Visible Spectroscopy (UV-Vis) focuses on absorption in the UV and visible light regions, commonly used for studying electronic transitions in molecules. Each technique serves distinct analytical needs across industries.</p></p>
