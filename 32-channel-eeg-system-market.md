<p><p>The 32-channel EEG system market is categorized into three main types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG records brain electrical activity through 32 electrodes, primarily for diagnosing disorders like epilepsy. Video EEG combines EEG monitoring with video recordings to capture clinical events, aiding in more accurate diagnosis and management. Dynamic EEG involves long-term monitoring, often in a patient's natural environment, allowing for a comprehensive assessment of brain function and seizure activity outside clinical settings.</p></p>
